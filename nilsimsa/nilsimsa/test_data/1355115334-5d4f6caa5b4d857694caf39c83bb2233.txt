Shane Mercer

   With the blowing snow and cold temps, it feels like winter is finally really here. And we'd like to see your winter photos. Email your snowy, frosty, frigid pics to                                    web@forumcomm.com    , and you might get to see them in print, online or on our Facebook page.    
       
   Stay warm and happy photographing!
